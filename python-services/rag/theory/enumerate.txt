  1. enumerate(results, 1)

  enumerate is a built-in that adds a counter to a loop.

  enumerate(results, 1)
  #                 ↑ start counting from 1 (default is 0)

  Without enumerate you'd need:
  i = 1
  for item in results:
      i += 1

  With enumerate — same thing, cleaner:
  for i, item in enumerate(results, 1):
      # i = 1, 2, 3...
