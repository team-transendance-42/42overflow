  User asks: "how does malloc work"
  BM25 finds documents that contain the words malloc, work — ranked by
  how rare+frequent those words are.

  It knows nothing about meaning. "allocate memory" scores zero even
  though it means the same thing. That's why you also have embeddings
  (the dense vector search) — embeddings catch meaning, BM25 catches
  exact words. Together they cover both cases.
